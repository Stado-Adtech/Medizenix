Quality & Compliance

At Medizenix Pharma, we follow strict quality control, legal compliance, and ethical distribution practices to ensure every product you receive is genuine, safe, and properly documented.

A. Our Quality Commitment

Trust, transparency, and authenticity

We are committed to supplying only genuine, legally sourced pharmaceutical products with proper documentation. Our operations ensure every dispatch meets regulatory requirements and industry best practices.

  • Only authorized and verified pharmaceutical brands are supplied
  • Strict checks to avoid fake, spurious, or unauthorized products
  • Proper purchase and sales documentation maintained
  • Transparent business practices with partners and customers
We do not deal in counterfeit, expired, or illegally sourced medicines. Compliance and patient safety come first.

C. Storage Standards

Safe handling & proper conditions

Clean & Organized Storage

Storage areas are kept clean and well-organized for different product categories.

Temperature Monitoring

Temperature is monitored where required to maintain product stability.

Pest Control

Regular pest control and hygiene practices are followed.

Safe Packing

D. Documentation Practices

Clear, compliant & transparent
  • Proper tax invoices are issued for all supplies
  • Batch number and expiry details maintained where applicable
  • Records kept for audit and compliance
  • Clear product and quantity details on invoices
This ensures transparency and helps buyers maintain compliance records.

E. Returns / Breakage / Expiry Policy

Simple, fair & transparent

What We Accept

  • Transit-damaged goods (within agreed time)
  • Incorrect supplies
  • Expiry cases as per agreement

How to Raise Request

  • Contact support team
  • Share invoice & product details
  • Provide photos if damaged

Timeline

  • Request within agreed time
  • Case reviewed by team
  • Resolution as per policy
Final acceptance is subject to company policy and product condition.
